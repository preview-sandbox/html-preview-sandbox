Catching iframe self-navigation from the main process

A sandboxed iframe can still try to navigate itself away with window.location = "…" — replacing your preview with, say, a phishing page. Pure Web can't reliably stop this. Electron's main process sees every frame navigation, cancels it, and the library re-mounts the original document. Try the two buttons in the preview and watch the log →
